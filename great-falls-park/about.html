Welcome to Great Falls Park (Virginia)

Discover Nature’s Majesty at Great Falls Park

Nestled along the Potomac River, Great Falls Park offers a breathtaking landscape filled with stunning waterfalls, scenic trails, and abundant wildlife. Whether you're here to hike, enjoy a peaceful picnic, or explore the historic remnants of the canal system, Great Falls has something for everyone. Located just outside Washington, D.C., it’s a perfect getaway into nature, blending rich history with spectacular outdoor experiences

Park History

Rich in History

Great Falls Park has been a vital part of the area's history for centuries. The park is home to the remains of the Chesapeake and Ohio Canal, a key transportation route built in the 1800s. The park was established to preserve the beauty and history of the area and became a National Park in 1966. You can explore the historical Lockhouse 10 or learn about the canal's role in shaping the region at the park's Visitor Center.
